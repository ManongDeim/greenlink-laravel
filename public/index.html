Events

Imagine a serene evening where the sky transforms into a canvas of glowing dreams. The Sky Lantern Parade is a magical event where participants gather to release lanterns, each carrying hopes, dreams, and wishes.

Participants light their lanterns, and with a gentle release, the night sky comes alive with floating lights, creating a scene straight out of a fairytale. The lanterns, made from eco-friendly materials, drift upwards, illuminating the darkness with their warm glow. This enchanting spectacle not only symbolizes the release of personal aspirations but also fosters a sense of community and shared experience.

The parade is often accompanied by live music, captivating performances, and delicious local food, making it a perfect blend of cultural celebration and personal reflection.
